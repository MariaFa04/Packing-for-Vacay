General Necessities

  • When you’re packing your clothes into your suitcase, make sure to remember to bring an extra one as well for all your soiled [&] wet clothes, making it easier for you to keep everything organized.
  • Since you are vacationing in sunny Florida, it is crucial not to pack any sort of hair spray or other aerosol containers in your luggage. The cause of the extreme heat, which can cause these items to explode, ruining everything else in the suitcase.
  • Pack a small sewing kit, including scissors to snip stray threads on clothing or at least take along a few safety pins and a needle with some thread attached. It’s also important to not lose the needle in the vacation pack.
  • Remember to always leave a little bit of space in your suitcase so that you have some space for potential souvenir purchases.
  • Don’t forget the prescription drugs you are likely to need in case of any emergencies. In addition, bring your eyeglass prescription in case your glasses get broken or lost.
